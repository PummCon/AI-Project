About Us

This website was created as our final project for COMM 408: AI in Human Communication, taught by Josh Pasek. In this class, we learned about how generative AI tools function and their limitations. We explored the societal implications of AI technologies in various domains and analyzed the ethical, legal and social challenges posed by AI. We created this website in order to give others who are unable to take this class the opportunity to learn more about how AI might impact future educational and job landscapes.
